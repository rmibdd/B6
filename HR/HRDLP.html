Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

The Human Resources Department is committed to attracting, developing, and retaining top talent that drives organizational success. We foster a positive workplace culture, ensure compliance with employment policies, and provide strategic support to enhance employee engagement, professional development, and overall organizational excellence.

Our Vision

To be a transformational HR partner that empowers employees to achieve their full potential while supporting the Rayomar Group's strategic objectives. We envision a workplace where talent is valued, diversity is celebrated, and continuous learning drives innovation and organizational growth.

Divisions

Talent Acquisition Division

The Recruitment & Talent Acquisition section is responsible for sourcing, screening, and hiring qualified candidates to meet the organization's staffing needs. This team conducts job analyses, develops recruitment strategies, and manages the entire hiring process from job posting through onboarding. They work closely with department heads to understand talent requirements and maintain a pipeline of high-quality candidates that align with the company's values and career development opportunities.

Beyond traditional recruitment, this section also focuses on employer branding and building relationships with educational institutions and professional networks. They work to create a positive candidate experience and ensure that selected employees are well-integrated into the organization through comprehensive onboarding programs.

Shared Services

The Shared Services Division is responsible for delivering centralized administrative and operational support functions across the organization. This division ensures the efficient management of core HR services such as employee records administration, payroll coordination, benefits processing, and policy implementation. By standardizing processes and maintaining compliance with company policies and regulatory requirements, the team promotes operational consistency and service excellence throughout all departments.

Beyond transactional support, the Shared Services Division continuously works to improve service delivery through process optimization, digitalization initiatives, and data-driven reporting. They serve as a primary point of contact for employee inquiries, ensuring timely resolution of concerns while enhancing overall employee experience and organizational efficiency.

Learning & Development

The Learning & Development Division is responsible for enhancing employee capabilities by designing and implementing structured training programs aligned with organizational goals. This division conducts training needs analysis, develops competency frameworks, and facilitates learning interventions that support both technical skills and leadership development. Their initiatives aim to strengthen workforce performance, productivity, and long-term talent sustainability.

In addition to formal training programs, the division promotes a culture of continuous learning through workshops, seminars, e-learning platforms, and career development pathways.

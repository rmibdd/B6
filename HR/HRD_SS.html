HR Department Divisions

Recruitment & Talent Acquisition

The Recruitment & Talent Acquisition section is responsible for sourcing, screening, and hiring qualified candidates. This team conducts job analyses, develops recruitment strategies, and manages the entire hiring process from job posting through onboarding.

Learn More
Training & Employee Development

The Training & Employee Development section ensures employees have the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in their roles. This includes identifying training needs, designing programs, and managing both technical and soft skills development.

Learn More
Employee Relations & Engagement

The Employee Relations & Engagement section focuses on building and maintaining positive relationships between the organization and its employees. They handle grievances, create engagement programs, and foster a positive workplace culture.

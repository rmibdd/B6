COMPANY PROFILE

The Rayomar group was founded in 1980 by our Chairman, Ramon C. Garcia. Today, Rayomar Management has seen the birth of several companies and joint ventures in the shipping, logistics, and trading industries. Such is the "Spirit of Enterprise"– seizing opportunities, creating value, and overcoming difficulties. This is what our group is about.

Through the years, Team Rayomar has been painstakingly built with a focus on doing things Right, as well as doing the Right things. We believe in investing in our people as they are our engine for growth. We believe in playing as a team and playing to win, but not at all costs. We play by the rules! And we believe that excellence is a process, a moving target, and that mediocrity is our enemy.

These beliefs, founded on integrity, business excellence, and entrepreneurial leadership, are the hallmarks of our team's success. With gratefulness and humility, we look forward to many more years of growth and opportunity. It is to these ends– the inspiration to serve our customers, provide greater value to our business partners, and care for our employees, that we dedicate our work over the coming years. Their success is our success!
